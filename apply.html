Section 5

Meetings & Fundraising Commitment

The training meetings for this mission trip are critical as well as mandatory. The meetings, which may be virtual or in person, are designed to ensure success of the mission. The meetings will begin in September. In addition, each team member will be required to attend three (3) fundraisers during the season leading up to the trip. Funds raised will be used exclusively for food donations in Peru. Failure to attend scheduled events will disqualify the applicant from the trip. Please confirm your agreement to commit faithfully to meetings and fundraising events.

If you are selected, we will require a $150 non-refundable application fee to hold the date. The application fee will be deducted from your final balance.

Section 10

Financial Notice of Understanding

  • It is my responsibility to secure necessary finances for the mission trip.
  • Trip balance must be paid 60 days before the scheduled trip.
  • Cancellation policy: Application fee of $150 is non-refundable. Application fee is due once the applicant has been accepted.
  • If cancelled 30 days or more before the trip — 50% refund.
  • If cancelled 21–29 days before the trip — 40% refund.
  • If cancelled 14–20 days before the trip — 30% refund.
  • If cancelled 0–13 days before the trip — 0% refund.
  • Any funds that have been raised by the attendee through fundraising or mission events will not be returned in cash. They may be used for another trip or designated for mission use. The funds will have one year to be used; if not, they are forfeited to the mission.
  • Extenuating circumstances (illness or the death of a family member) for trip cancellation will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.
  • Refunds for airfare are determined by LATAM Airlines — please refer to your ticket.
  • I understand that the team leader reserves the right to ask me to return home if my behavior is destructive to the team, the ministry, or the host community. The following warrant an immediate return home: alcohol use, drug use, or violation of Peruvian laws. Any additional cost incurred as a result of this action will be my responsibility. Sembradores de Semillas Mission Trips is not responsible to reimburse my trip expenses.
